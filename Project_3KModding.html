Northern Barbarians: Mod Development, for Three Kingdoms

Northern Barbarians is a mod that I made in my spare time for Total War Three Kingdoms and is available on the Steam Workshop.
I led the Design and Developement from concept to launch on the Steam Workshop.

  • Link to Project

My Role included:

  • Full design of factions their mechanics and effects.
  • Scripting of missions/events/dilemmas and progression.
  • Design of characters, items and their effects.
  • Design for unique faction buildings and effects.
  • Design of unique faction battle units and balancing

My Design Process

Ideation

When first starting this project I started with asking questions about what players wanted/expected this led to the creation of the Goals and Pillars that I could use to guide me through development. With this I was able to better inform my research to fit my Goals and Pillars which are below.

  1. Life of the nomad everything must be useful nothing is wasted
  2. Fear I must make my enemies fear me to get what I want
  3. Opportunity The timing must be perfect or I risk losing everything

Research & Documentation

With clear goals set, development was able to proceed starting with research for factions and characters to add to the project. After research I started prioritising characters that where must haves as they would be characters that players would recognise and learning how to add them in-engine. After the core characters where added I moved to designing and prototyping the faction mechanics in-engine which was core to not only the gameplay loop but also the player experiece. After first implementation I conducted a small feedback session to see if my player experience goals were being achieved and iterating where needed. This proved vital and allowed for further balancing before release.

After Both Charcters and Faction Mechanics etc had been added then the focussed my attention on progression and implementation of a narrative for the faction which I had roughly laid out in my inital pass, but this needed fleshing out further to create a more compelling experience.
