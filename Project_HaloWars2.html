Halo Wars 2 + DLCS

On Halo wars 2 I was primarily the key contact for AI related systems for QA. .

My Responsibilities included:

  • Designing and implementation of AI agents for DLC levels “Operation Spearbreaker” through text-based XML's.
  • Consultation and Preliminary designs for AI agents for DLC “Awakening the Nightmare” Missions.
  • Working with other designers on needs and requirements for AI agents for levels/missions.
  • Creation of testplans for features
  • Testing of all AI behaviours both in campaign and skirmish mode
  • Writing of AI documentation and teaching to other QA members
