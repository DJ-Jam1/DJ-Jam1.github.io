The Rhythm of Pallissery

This was a project that I worked on with friends as part of a Game Jam in which we were given 3 themes and created a visual novel rhythm based game. In which you read through the story and then play through increasingly challenging Rhythm based puzzles on your way to the final stage in Pallissery India.
